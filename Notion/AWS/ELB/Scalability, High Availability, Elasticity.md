- Scalability means that an application can handle greater loads by adapting, ability to accommodate a large load by making the hardware stronger, or by adding nodes
1. Vertical Scaling: increase instance size (scale up/down)
    - From t2.nano - 0.5 G of RAM, 1vCPU
    - To u-12tbl.metal - 12.3 TB of Ram, 448vCPUs
2. Horizontal Scaling: increase number of instances (scale out/in)
    - Auto Scaling Group
    - Load Balancer

- High Availability means running the application in at least two Availability Zones , the goal is to survive a data center loss
- Elasticity means that the ability of application can “auto-scaling” based on the loads
