**Amazon Rekognition** is a fully managed ML service for **image and video analysis**.

It can automatically detect **objects, people, text, activities, and scenes** using deep learning.

---

## **✨ Key Capabilities**

- **Label Detection**
Identify objects, scenes, and activities in images/videos.
- **Content Moderation**
Detect inappropriate or unsafe content.
- **Text Detection (OCR)**
Extract text from images (e.g., signs, documents).
- **Face Detection & Analysis**
Detect faces and analyze attributes such as:
    - Gender
    - Age range
    - Emotions
    - Landmarks (eyes, nose, etc.)
- **Face Search & Verification**
    - Compare faces against a **collection** (face database).
    - Useful for identity verification, matching, or counting people.
- **Celebrity Recognition**
Identify well-known celebrities.
- **Pathing (Tracking)**
Track the movement of people in video frames.

---

## **💡 Rekognition — Exam Tips**

- Use Rekognition for **image/video analysis**, not text documents (→ Textract).
- Use Rekognition for **face comparison or face search**.
- Use Rekognition for **unsafe content moderation**.

# **204. Amazon Transcribe — Overview**

**Amazon Transcribe** converts **speech to text** using deep learning (ASR: Automatic Speech Recognition).

Upload audio → receive accurate, timestamped transcripts.

---

## **✨ Key Features**

- **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)**
High-quality transcription for audio files.
- **PII Redaction**
Automatically remove sensitive information such as:
    - Names
    - Addresses
    - SSN
    - Phone numbers
- **Automatic Language Identification**
Detects languages in multilingual audio files.
- **Custom Vocabulary**
Improve accuracy for domain-specific terms (brand names, technical jargon).
- **Timestamps Per Word**
Useful for subtitles or syncing audio and text.

---

## **🎤 Common Use Cases**

- Transcribing **customer service calls**
- **Closed captioning** and **subtitles** for videos
- Building **searchable archives** of audio content
- Voice analytics workflows (call centers)

---

## **💡 Transcribe — Exam Tips**

- Use Transcribe for **speech → text** conversion.
- For handwriting or scanned documents → **Textract** (not Transcribe).
- For language translation → **Translate**.
- For conversational analytics → **Contact Lens** (within Amazon Connect).
