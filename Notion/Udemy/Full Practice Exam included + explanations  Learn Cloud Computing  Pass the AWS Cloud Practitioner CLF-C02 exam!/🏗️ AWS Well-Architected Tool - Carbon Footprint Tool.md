**CLF-002**

---

## 🎯 What is it? (Exam-friendly)

The **AWS Well-Architected Tool** helps you **review workloads** against the **six pillars** of the AWS Well-Architected Framework and **identify architectural improvements**.

> Purpose:
> **Assess → Identify risks → Get best-practice recommendations**

---

## 🧩 How It Works (Simple Flow)

```plain text
Select workload
   ↓
Answer pillar-based questions
   ↓
Review resultsper pillar
   ↓
Receive recommendations
```

---

## 🧱 What It Reviews

The tool evaluates your workload against **all 6 pillars**:

1. Operational Excellence
2. Security
3. Reliability
4. Performance Efficiency
5. Cost Optimization
6. Sustainability

---

## 📄 Output & Benefits

- Best-practice **recommendations**
- **Risk identification**
- Supporting:
    - Documentation
    - Videos
    - Reports

> Exam keyword:
> **Architectural guidance & continuous improvement**

---

## 🧠 CLF-002 Exam Notes

- Used for **architecture review**, not deployment
- Helps improve designs **over time**
- Aligns workloads with AWS **best practices**

### Typical Exam Question

> Q: How do you evaluate an architecture against AWS best practices?
> ✅ A: Use the **AWS Well-Architected Tool**

---

## 🧩 One-Sentence Exam Answer

> The AWS Well-Architected Tool helps review workloads against the six pillars and provides best-practice recommendations.

# 🌍 AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool

**CLF-002 | Sustainability**

---

## 🎯 What is it?

The **AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool** is used to:

- **Track**
- **Measure**
- **Review**
- **Forecast**

the **carbon emissions** generated by your AWS usage.

---

## 🌱 Why It Matters

- Supports **sustainability goals**
- Helps organizations monitor environmental impact
- Enables data-driven sustainability decisions

---

## 📊 What You Can See

- Total **carbon emissions**
- **Carbon savings**
- Emissions **by AWS service**
- Emissions **over time**
- Progress toward **100% renewable energy**

---

## 🧠 CLF-002 Exam Notes

- Focused on **environmental impact**, not cost
- Strongly tied to the **Sustainability pillar**
- Useful for **reporting & forecasting emissions**

### Exam Trap

- ❌ Cost Explorer → cost only
- ✅ Carbon Footprint Tool → **emissions**

---

## 🧩 One-Sentence Exam Answer

> The AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool helps customers track, analyze, and forecast carbon emissions from their AWS usage.
