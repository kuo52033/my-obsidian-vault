## **1. What is Consolidated Billing?**

Consolidated Billing is a feature of **AWS Organizations** that provides:

### **✔ Combined Usage Across All Accounts**

- AWS aggregates usage from all member accounts.
- This enables:
    - **Volume discounts** across S3, EC2, data transfer, etc.
    - **RI (Reserved Instance) and Savings Plans discount sharing**

### **✔ One Single Bill**

- Billing is handled by the **Management Account**.
- Member accounts do *not* need separate payment methods.
- Great for finance departments and cost management.

---

## **2. Combined Usage Benefits**

### **🔹 Volume Pricing Discounts**

Example (S3 tiered pricing):

- First 5 TB → $X
- Next 45 TB → cheaper price

If you have:

- 6 accounts each using 1 TB
→ Individually: no discount
→ Consolidated: 6 TB total → qualifies for cheaper tier

**Exam Tip:** “Aggregated usage” = better pricing.

---

## **3. Reserved Instance (RI) & Savings Plans Sharing**

### **How RI sharing works**

- RI discounts can apply **across all accounts** inside the organization.
- Applies automatically *unless explicitly disabled*.

### **Example Scenario**

Organization has:

- **Account A**: No RIs
- **Account B**: 5 Reserved EC2 Instances
- Total running EC2 instances (same AZ): 9
    - Account B runs 3
    - Account A runs 6

### **How discounts apply:**

- First, cover Account B’s own 3 instances.
- 2 remaining reserved instances apply to 2 instances in Account A.

### **Final result:**

- **5 instances billed at RI price**
- **4 instances billed at On-Demand price**

```plain text
Total EC2 instances: 9
Reserved Instances: 5
Covered: 3 (B) + 2 (A) = 5
Uncovered: 4


```

### **Important Exam Note**

- **RI sharing can be disabled per account**, even for the management account.

---

## **4. Why Companies Use Consolidated Billing**

- Cost savings through shared discounts
- Centralized financial management
- Simplified accounting and chargeback
- Encourages multi-account best practices without additional billing overhead

---

## **5. Exam Tips (High Yield)**

- Consolidated billing is part of **AWS Organizations**, not standalone.
- Combined usage → **volume discounts**.
- **RI & Savings Plans discounts are shared by default**.
- Sharing can be disabled.
- One bill for all accounts.
- Does **NOT** merge resources—only merges billing.
